Exec summary

Executive summary is used to give a high level idea of your findings. A really nice thing you can do, if you use the deck.hash extension, is that you can make links within your presentation.

I've made a few divs to present content

This div has a 'small-left' class. It matches up with the 'big-right' div. The 'big-right' div on this slide has my sin plot in it.

This div is useful if you have a plot that you want to display in lots of detail, with a little commentary on the other side.

I've found that you need to put your images in a 'div.deck-container' to make them line up correctly.

There is also a corresponding 'small-right' and 'big-left' pair of divs.
